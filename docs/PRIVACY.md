# YumiShare — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 6 July 2026
**Contact:** support@yumishare.com

This Privacy Policy explains what data YumiShare ("we", "the app") collects, how
we use it, and the third parties we share it with. By using the app you agree to
this policy.

## 1. Data we collect

- **Account data.** When you sign up we store your email address and an account
  identifier. You can sign in with email, Apple, or Google.
- **Content you create or import.** Recipes you import or add, cookbooks,
  grocery lists, meal plans, pantry items, and scanned receipts.
- **Images.** Photos you take or select as recipe/cookbook covers, and photos of
  recipes, food labels, or receipts that you choose to scan.
- **Links you import.** URLs you paste from Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest,
  YouTube, Facebook, or other websites so we can extract the recipe.
- **Usage data.** Basic technical data needed to operate the service (e.g. app
  version, error diagnostics).

We do **not** sell your personal data.

## 2. How we use your data

We use your data to provide the app's features: extracting recipes from links
and images, storing your library, generating grocery lists and meal plans,
tracking receipts, and managing your subscription.

## 3. AI processing (important)

YumiShare uses **OpenAI** to turn the content you import into structured recipes
and to read receipts and food labels. When you import a link or scan an image,
the relevant content — **post captions, page text, recipe images, receipt
photos, and food-label photos** — is transmitted to OpenAI for automated
analysis and classification. We only send this content when you actively trigger
an import or scan. You are asked to consent to this on first launch.

## 4. Third parties we share data with

- **OpenAI, L.L.C.** — AI extraction of recipes, receipts, and labels (see §3).
- **Supabase** — secure storage of your account, content, and images.
- **Open Food Facts** — barcode lookups (we send the scanned barcode number).
- **RevenueCat and Apple / Google** — subscription purchase and status.

Each provider processes data under its own privacy policy.

## 5. Data retention and account deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can **delete your account at
any time** from **Profile → Delete Account** in the app. Deleting your account
permanently removes your account and associated data from our systems.

## 6. Security

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS) and stored with access
controls. Private keys and administrative credentials are held only on our
backend server and are never included in the mobile app.

## 7. Children

YumiShare is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect
data from them.

## 8. Your rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or
delete your data. Contact us at support@yumishare.com to exercise them.

## 9. Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by a new effective
date at the top of this page.

## 10. Contact

Questions about this policy: **support@yumishare.com**
